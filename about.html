Why Shanti Bhagwati Secondary School

With great mission this school has been running English medium classes. Its main objective is to impart qualitative education along with the implementation of practical knwoledge so that the students should not have to go away out of society in the name of quality education. This school stresses more in maintaining disciple besides providing quality education.

Aims and objectives of this school

  1. To make Learning more effective and joyful.
  2. To develop the habits of clean and healthy living.
  3. To develop sense of values, duty, punctuality, obedience, respect, co-operation, discipline and leadership in a society.
  4. To ensure mental, moral, physical and emotional development of every child who may be actively participated and dedicated in self service to be 21thcentury competitor.
  5. To lead society with exemplary work & functions.
